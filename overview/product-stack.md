# Product stack

Trugate is structured as a portfolio of products that build on one another.

## Product 1 — Trugate Invoice

The first operational product.

Purpose:
- capture invoices,
- validate them,
- submit them for fiscal clearance,
- generate trusted digital invoice records.

## Product 2 — Trugate Business Passport

Purpose:
- create reusable trust profiles for businesses,
- make verified business identity portable,
- support counterpart verification.

## Product 3 — Trugate Trade Ready

Purpose:
- transform trusted invoice and identity data into customs-compatible and export-ready data artifacts.

## Product 4 — Trugate Finance Enablement

Purpose:
- allow financial systems to act on verified transaction data.

## Why the stack matters

Each product depends on the trust generated by the previous layer. Trugate therefore grows from compliance into broader digital trade infrastructure.
