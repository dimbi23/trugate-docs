# What is Trugate

Trugate is a **digital trust infrastructure platform** designed to transform commercial transactions into trusted digital trade assets.

Its purpose is to connect the systems that govern commercial reality:
- trade,
- fiscality,
- customs,
- and later finance.

Trugate does not aim to be a full accounting suite or ERP. Its role is to sit between business systems and institutional systems, making transactions:
- compliant,
- verifiable,
- portable,
- and reusable across domains.

## Why Trugate exists

In many markets, businesses must separately interact with tax systems, customs systems, banks, and internal software. The same transaction is recreated multiple times in different formats, which causes:
- manual work,
- delays,
- inconsistencies,
- fraud risk,
- and limited access to digital services.

Trugate reduces this fragmentation by creating a **single trusted transaction flow**.

## The Trugate approach

Trugate begins with the most universal commercial object: the invoice.

A normal invoice is usually a private business document. Through Trugate, that invoice can become:
- validated,
- cleared,
- archived,
- machine-readable,
- and reusable by external systems.

That is why **Invoice** is the first Trugate product.
