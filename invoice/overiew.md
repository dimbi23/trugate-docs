# Trugate Invoice overview

Trugate Invoice is the first product in the Trugate platform.

It is a compliance gateway that enables businesses and software systems to issue **DGI-cleared electronic invoices** through a dashboard or API.

## What it does

Trugate Invoice helps users:
- create invoice data,
- validate required information,
- convert data into compliant electronic format,
- submit the invoice to the fiscal clearance process,
- receive the clearance result,
- generate a PDF with verification data,
- archive the final document.

## Who it is for

Trugate e-Invoice is designed for:
- SMEs issuing invoices regularly,
- accounting firms managing multiple companies,
- ERP and invoicing platforms,
- marketplaces and transaction systems.

## What problem it solves

Many businesses do not want to handle:
- XML/UBL formatting,
- tax validation logic,
- DGI submission flow,
- invoice status reconciliation,
- QR and archive generation.

Trugate absorbs that complexity.
