# Compliance

TruGate e-Invoice exists to make electronic invoicing compliance operational and understandable.

## Compliance scope

The product must support:
- fiscal identity verification,
- mandatory invoice field validation,
- tax logic checks,
- compliant digital formatting,
- trusted archive and auditability.

## Key compliance principles

### 1. Validate before submission
The system should catch missing or inconsistent data before sending anything downstream.

### 2. Preserve auditability
Every important event in the invoice lifecycle should be traceable.

### 3. Keep legal and user-facing forms aligned
The machine-readable invoice and the downloadable PDF should represent the same trusted transaction.

### 4. Support role accountability
The system should make it possible to know who created, submitted, or changed invoice data.

## Future compliance extensions

Over time, TruGate can extend this compliance layer into:
- business trust credentials,
- customs-compatible transaction flows,
- finance-grade proof of trade.
