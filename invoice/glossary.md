# Glossary

## Clearance
The process through which an invoice is validated and recognized by the fiscal system.

## Cleared invoice
An invoice that has successfully passed validation and clearance and can be treated as a trusted record.

## Compliance engine
The TruGate service responsible for validating invoice structure and rules.

## NIF
Taxpayer identifier used for business identification.

## SAFI
The fiscal clearance environment referenced by TruGate e-Invoice workflows.

## Trusted transaction record
A structured digital representation of a commercial transaction that has been validated and can be reused across domains.

## UBL
Universal Business Language, a common structured format for business documents.
